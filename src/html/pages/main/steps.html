How It Works

Step-by-Step Process

  • Choose Your Service Option

    Free Pick-Up and Drop-Off Service

    We will arrange to pick up your baby gear from your location, clean and sanitize it at our facility, and return it to you.

    On-Site Service

    Our professionals will come to your location, set up our mess-free cleaning station, and perform the cleaning and sanitizing on-site.

  • Schedule Your Service

    Book online or call us to select your preferred service option and schedule a convenient time for our team to visit.

  • Cleaning and Sanitizing

    For Pick-Up and Drop-Off:

    We transport your items to our facility where they undergo thorough cleaning and steam sanitizing.

    For On-Site Service

    We clean and steam sanitize your baby gear at your location using eco-friendly products.

  • Quality Check

    We conduct a final inspection to ensure everything meets our high standards.

  • Enjoy

    Your baby gear is now clean, sanitized, and ready for use!
